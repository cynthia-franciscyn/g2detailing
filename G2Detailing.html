What We Do

Built Around Your Car's Needs

From a quick refresh before a long drive to a full paint correction, every service is carried out by hand with studio-grade products.

01

Full Body Wash

Two-bucket hand wash with pH-neutral shampoo and a contactless dry to keep swirl marks off your paint.

02

Auto Detailing

A complete top-to-bottom detail covering exterior, interior, wheels, and trim — restored to a like-new finish.

03

Car Waxing

A protective wax layer that deepens shine and shields your paint from sun, dust, and minor abrasions.

04

Wheel Washing

Brake-dust and grime removed from rims and wells, finished with a tyre dressing for a clean, matte-black look.

05

Exhaust Tip Polishing

Tarnish and soot buffed off exhaust tips and chrome trim, brought back to a mirror finish.

06

Headlight Polishing

Wet-sanding and polishing to clear out yellowed, foggy headlights — restoring brightness and night visibility.

07

Vehicle Interior Vacuuming

Thorough vacuuming of seats, carpets, mats, and tight corners to lift out dust, sand, and debris.

08

Seat Shampooing

Deep-cleaned fabric and leather seats, lifting stains and odours for a fresh, hygienic cabin.

About Us

Detailing Done
the Patient Way

G2 Detailing studio is a car care studio in Kundrathur, Chennai, run by Ranjan and Karthik — built on a simple idea: take the time most quick-washes skip. Every vehicle that comes through our doors, car or bike, gets inspected, treated, and finished by hand.

We're not a drive-through wash. We're a studio for owners who want their car to look the way it did on day one — and to keep looking that way.
